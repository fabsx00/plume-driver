PlumeGraph

class PlumeGraph: Any

Represents a CPG where vertices are PlumeVertex instances and edges are categorized by their EdgeLabel under a HashMap which then returns the source PlumeVertex and a HashSet of all PlumeVertexs connected by the EdgeLabel.
